Our Games

Hoagie Platformer

One of our most popular video games is Hoagie Platformer, a platformer where YOU take the point of view of a hoagie and explore the wonders of life. Gather more toppings to get a higher score and become the best hoagie out there.

Doodle Wars

The game that most of our time is spent on right now is Doodle Wars. Your job is to protect the castle and crush all opposition that stands in the way of your path to glory. Originally starting as a paper game, this is a game that you are sure to put hours into.

City Bombing

This unique game is set up from a bird's eye view, were you can move arround the city as you please, and blowing up anything you see. A fun and relaxing game to pass the time, and it is currently being worked on now.
